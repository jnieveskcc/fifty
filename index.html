The Power of Community

You know our grads

KCC’s mission is Enhancing Quality of Life through Learning. It says so much about what we provide for our community. With more than 14,000 graduates since 1970, you likely know our alumni.

They educate our children, care for us when we’re sick, tell our stories, keep order, help others, and build our economy. We call it the Power of Community. In fact, that’s our tag line this year as we celebrate our 50th anniversary.
